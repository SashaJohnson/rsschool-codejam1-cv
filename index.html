Motivation

Ever since I entered this world, I've always been a curious person. No wonder my life has been a captivating journey so far - I've been to many places, switched different career paths, eventually ending up as a business analyst with one of the leading software providers.

Being a BA requires one to live on the boundary between two worlds: the world of business, people, communication and that of technology, machinery and code.

To enhance my knowledge of the technical realm, recently, I've signed up for a FE course provided by The Rolling Scopes School. During the training, I'm hoping to acquire a better understanding of software architecture, fill the gaps, and put myself in the shoes of an engineer to see their way of tackling problems 😊
